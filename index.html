Shopping experiences

Have you ever had doubts when buying from some distant place or country? Can you trust in that small online store? Do you have any idea about how much tax you have to pay for your imported goods? Sometimes is hard to make a shopping decision!

Our platform will help you with this and many more issues when purchasing! The goal of our business is to support our users to facilitate their shopping experiences and avoid any headaches.

How it works?

ShopAdvisr is a web platform that allows its users to make requests for information about stores (physical or online) in different parts of the world. Our on-site agents will conduct an investigation on such stores and will provide a reliability report that will allow our customers to make a shopping decision.

If the purchase is made from a foreign country, the report will include information about the shipping methods, taxes and other issues involved.

FAQ

How much will you charge for your services?
We'll charge just a small fee for our services. When you request for information, the form will calculate in real time how much you could pay for our services. We'll charge you just the information delivered.
Can you make some shopping for me?
We don't make direct shopping for our customers; but if you're in a hurry, we could figure out something to help you.
Can I work with you as an adviser?
Yes! We'll be glad to have you with us! We're looking for people that can help us with the advising in many cities around the world! Please contact us for information about this.
